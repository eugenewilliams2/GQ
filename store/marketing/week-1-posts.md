### Posting tips
- **Hooks win.** The first 3 words / first 3 seconds decide everything. If a post flops, the hook was weak — not the product.
- **Always pin** your best-performing post and keep the link in every bio.
- **Reply to every comment** in the first hour — it boosts reach and opens DM sales.
- Use the **AI Marketing Studio** on your site to generate fresh variations when you run dry.
