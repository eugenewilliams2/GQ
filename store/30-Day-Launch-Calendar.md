# 30-Day Launch Calendar — 70 AI Specialists for Claude

A done-for-you posting plan to take the product from $0 to first sales. Built to run on accounts **you** own (IG, TikTok, X, optionally a short email list).

**How to use it**
- Each day: 1 piece of content. Batch-make a week at a time (Sunday = make 7).
- Use the **AI Marketing Studio** on your site (`index.html`) to spin up captions fast.
- The funnel is simple: **content → free Studio on the site → $57 product.**
- Pin your best post. Put the site link in every bio.

**The 4 content angles (rotate them):**
1. **Pain** — name the expensive/slow problem (hiring help).
2. **Proof** — show a specialist doing real work (screen-recordings convert best).
3. **Value** — give away one specialist or tip for free.
4. **Offer** — direct pitch + CTA.

---

## Week 1 — Plant the flag (build curiosity)

**Day 1 · IG/TikTok Reel · Pain**
Hook: *"Freelancers quoted me $3,000/month. So I built a team for $57."*
Show the device/cover, then text-on-screen of the 9 categories. CTA: "Link in bio."

**Day 2 · X/Threads · Value**
Post one full specialist prompt (e.g. the **Headline Specialist**) as free value. End: "I have 69 more like this 👀"

**Day 3 · IG Carousel · Proof**
5 slides: "I asked Claude to write my sales page using 1 of my 70 specialists." Show before (blank) → after (copy). Slide 5 = offer + link.

**Day 4 · TikTok · Value**
Screen-record yourself pasting the **Performance Marketer** prompt and getting a real plan. "This is 1 of 70."

**Day 5 · X · Pain**
"You don't need to hire a:
- copywriter
- marketer
- analyst
- closer
You need 70 prompts and 20 minutes." + link.

**Day 6 · IG Story (3 frames) · Offer**
Poll: "Would you pay $57 once for 70 AI experts?" → result frame → swipe-up/link.

**Day 7 · Recap post · Proof**
"7 days, here's everything 70 AI Specialists replaced for me." List + link. Pin this.

## Week 2 — Show the work (proof > promises)

**Day 8 · Reel · Proof** — Screen-record the **Sales Closer** handling a real objection. "Tell me your objection in the comments, I'll run it."

**Day 9 · X thread · Value** — "7 prompts that do the work of a $5k/mo marketer 🧵" Give 3 mini-versions, link for all 70.

**Day 10 · TikTok · Pain** — Duet/stitch a "hustle is hard" video. "Or… use this." Show the pack.

**Day 11 · IG Carousel · Value** — "Steal my 5 favorite specialists." Tease names + 1 full prompt. CTA for the rest.

**Day 12 · X · Offer** — "70 AI Specialists. $57 once. No subscription. Here's everything inside 👇" Screenshot the category list + link.

**Day 13 · Reel · Proof** — "I let Claude run my whole content day with these prompts." Time-lapse. Link.

**Day 14 · Email #1 (if you have a list) · Value** — Subject: "An entire team for the price of dinner." Give 1 specialist free, link to buy the 70.

## Week 3 — Turn up the offer (handle objections)

**Day 15 · Reel · Offer** — "Why it's $57 and not a subscription." Honesty sells. Link.

**Day 17 · TikTok · Value** — "POV: you found the prompt that writes your ads." Show **Paid Ads Copywriter**.
**Day 18 · IG Carousel · Pain** — "Things I stopped paying for after this." (freelance copy, ad agency, etc.) Link.
**Day 19 · Reel · Proof** — Show the **Music & Lyric Collaborator** writing a hook (great for your GQ GENO audience — cross-promote your music page).
**Day 20 · X thread · Value** — "10 ways I use Claude that feel illegal 🧵" Each tied to a specialist. Link at the end.
**Day 21 · Email #2 · Offer** — Subject: "I almost charged $540 for this." Frame value vs. price. Clear CTA.

## Week 4 — Push to first sales (scarcity that's TRUE)

> Only use real urgency. A genuine "launch price ends Sunday, then it goes up" is honest *if you actually raise it.* Never fake a countdown.

**Day 22 · Reel · Offer** — "Launch price ends this week — then it goes up." (Mean it.) Link.
**Day 23 · X · Proof** — "What 70 AI Specialists actually looks like." Screen-record scrolling the PDF. Link.
**Day 24 · TikTok · Value** — "The 1 prompt I'd keep if I could only keep one." Show it. "69 more in bio."
**Day 25 · IG Carousel · Pain→Offer** — "Stop doing $5k/mo work yourself." Walk the transformation. Link.
**Day 26 · Reel · Proof** — Customer results / your own dashboard / before-after. Link.
**Day 27 · X · Offer** — "Last chance at launch price. $57 → going up Monday." (Only if true.)
**Day 28 · Email #3 · Offer** — Subject: "Closes tonight." Final push, recap the offer + guarantee.

**Day 29 · Story countdown · Offer** — Real countdown sticker to the price change. Link in every frame.
**Day 30 · Recap + restart · Proof** — "Month 1 done. Here's what worked." Announce price is now higher / next drop. Pin. Then loop back to Day 1 with fresh hooks.

---

## After Day 30 — keep the engine running
- **Repurpose winners.** Any post that popped → remake it 3 different ways.
- **Comments → DMs → sales.** Reply to every comment; soft-pitch in DMs.
- **Add specialists.** Each new specialist = a free content piece + an "updated pack" reason to repost.
- **Collect testimonials.** One screenshot of a happy buyer outperforms 10 ads.
- **Track one number:** clicks to the site → buyers. If clicks are high but sales low, fix the offer/price. If clicks are low, fix the hooks.

*Honest note: this plan drives attention to a real product. Sales depend on consistency and your offer — not luck. Post daily for 30 days before judging it.*
